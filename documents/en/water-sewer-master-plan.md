# Water Supply and Sewerage Master Plan

Date: 05.05.2026  
Object: two-storey villa with accessible roof garden  
Location: Batumi, Georgia  
Building area: 303.42 m²  
Roof Garden: 108.50 m²  
Stage: rough water supply, sewerage, drainage and stormwater routes

## System Scope

| System | Scope |
|---|---|
| Cold water | entry, filtration, manifolds, distribution to points |
| Hot water | water heater/boiler unit, manifolds, distribution to points |
| DHW recirculation | recirculation loop, pump, check valves |
| Sewerage | risers, vent stacks, horizontal branches, cleanouts |
| AC condensate drainage | condensate collection from split/outdoor units, discharge to stormwater/drainage |
| Stormwater | drains, emergency overflows, pipes, outlet |
| Roof Garden drainage | slopes, drains, overflows, access points |
| External drainage | perimeter drainage, channels, outlet |

## Technical Unit

| Element | Execution |
|---|---|
| Water entry | shut-off valves, coarse filter, meter, pressure reducer |
| Cold water manifold | separate lines to bathrooms, kitchens, washrooms, roof garden |
| Hot water manifold | separate lines to bathrooms, kitchens, washrooms, roof garden |
| Boiler unit | storage or instantaneous water heater by load |
| Recirculation | pump, timer/automation, check valves |
| Drainage unit | condensate reception, traps, discharge to drainage/stormwater |
| Access | filters, manifolds, pumps, valves |
